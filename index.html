Computer Science meets Environment Science Workshop 2024

We are organizing an event sponsored by the Association for Computer Machinery (the world's largest scientific and educational computing society) under the auspices of ACM Sigmod ACM Sigmod (The ACM Special Interest Group on Management of Data) to promote cross-fertilization between Computer Science and Environmental Science communities as part of the ACM Europe Building Bridges. The ACM Europe Building Bridges initiative aims at gathering together people from different scientific communities, such as for instance a subfield of Computer Science (e.g., Data Management) and another scientific field (e.g. Environmental Sciences) in order to allow interactions and cross-fertilization between two subfields for future collaborations. This year, the theme of this event is "Computer Science meets Environmental Sciences: How Computer Science Can Help Save the Environment".

In particular, in this event, people from the Data Management and Environmental Sciences communities will get together to discuss about challenges and themes, such as (non-exhaustive, subjected to change based on the inputs from both communities):

  • Computational challenges of Environmental Sciences
    • (broader than Climate Sciences and has impact on Climate Change)
  • Challenges that the environmental sciences pose for Computer Science
  • Energy-efficient algorithms with Cloud infrastructures
  • Impact of environment on Public Health
  • How to make Computer Science more efficient to achieve less energy consumption
  • How computer Science can drive our efforts to conserve our Environment
  • Management of resources needed to solve the problems confronting sustainability
  • How to help Environmental Scientists in achieving their data storage and processing needs
  • Other topics of mutual interests
